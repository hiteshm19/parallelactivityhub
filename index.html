Parallel Activity Hub

We believe everyone deserves a parallel — a quiet, creative lane running alongside the rush of everyday life. We craft spaces where you can pause, make with your hands, and reconnect with yourself.

No noise. No pressure. Just presence. 🎨

How it works

Each workshop is hosted at a different venue across Dubai — cafés, gardens, and interesting spaces. All materials are included. You just show up, make something, and take it home.

Payment

We use Ziina for quick, secure payments. A payment link is shared with each workshop registration. Pay before the deadline to confirm your spot.
